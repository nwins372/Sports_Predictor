2026 NBA Draft Big Board 1.0 – Preseason Edition

October 26, 2025October 26, 2025 by NBA Draft Room

With the season just about to get underway here’s my first Top 70 Big Board for the 2026 NBA Draft cycle.

The top of my board is pretty much in-line with consensus, with the three top prospects (DP, AJ and Cam) followed by a handful of high level lottery talents. Players I’m higher on than consensus include Tounde Yessoufou, Tomislav Ivisic, Brayden Burries and Cayden Boozer.

I’ve included Miikka Muurinen on this big board since he will likely be draft eligible, although he’s stated that he’ll probably wait until 2027. The 6-11 Fin will be playing in the ABA this season for Partizan, Belgrade.

There’s always a lot of movement on the big board throughout the season but the biggest moves usually happen in the first weeks of the season as the prospects either hit the ground running or falter out of the gates. For now this is my top 70 prospects for the 2026 draft but expect some big changes when Big Board 2.0 gets released in about a month.

About The Tiers: I like ranking the prospects in tiers and have seven tiers on this big board (after having 8 tiers last year). Although the tiers help organize the board and give some context to the strength of the draft class the cut-offs between the tiers can be a bit arbitrary and will be fluid throughout the season.

2026 NBA Draft Big Board 1.0

The 2026 NBA draft class is clearly a very deep class but it doesnt totally hit home how deep it is until you try to cut players from a list like this. There are at least a dozen prospects that I would like to have on this top 70 big board. But thats the nature of the beast, hard decisions have to be made.

I ended up putting a number of freshmen on this list that could end up staying in school for a few years. If some of these youngsters get off to a slow start they might drop of the list and open up space for some more upperclassmen.

Stay tuned for another big board update coming a month or so into the college season.
